## 测试公式
### inlineMath
Test 1: inlineMath, code:```$1\alpha\$```,
test: $1\alpha$.

### displayMath
Test 2: displayMath without label, code: ```\[ \alpha = \sum_{i=1}^N \sigma_i \]```,
test: \[ \alpha = \sum_{i=1}^N \sigma_i.\]

Test 3: displayMath with label, 欧拉恒等式 \eqref{eq:Euler}, code: 
```tex
\begin{equation}
  e^{i\pi} + 1 =0.
  \label{eq:Euler}
\end{equation}
```
test:
\begin{equation}
  e^{i\pi} + 1 =0.
  \label{eq:Euler}
\end{equation}


Test 4: displayMath with label, code:
```tex
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{ll}
c^2 & = a^2-b^2 \\\\ !!!a whitespace at the end!!!
    & = (a+b)(a-b).
\label{eq:gougu}
\end{array}
\end{equation}
```
test: 
\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{ll}
c^2 & = a^2-b^2 \\\\ 
    & = (a+b)(a-b).
\label{eq:gougu}
\end{array}
\end{equation}
